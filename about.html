    Shipping

    Lookie's cookies ships cookies and gifts weekly from our barely in Michigan, Ann Arbor, on Mondays, Tuesdays and Wednesdays. As the cookies have no preservatives, all orders are shipped to arrive within two days to maintain freshness. If your order is received before 1:00pm Central Time (CT) on Monday,Tuesday or Wednesday, we will ship your order on that day or you'll be notified of any delays.

    Your order are shipped to arrive within two days of the requested ship date. During the ordering process, you can request a future ship date, or the calendar will automatically default to choosing the next available ship date. In order to ensure the freshest possible product, we do not hip products after Wednesday's so we can avoid our cookies sitting over the weekend.
